# GrowthSphere

## **Service Overview**

GrowthSphere is a platform where you can share what you want to learn and grow together with others. Whether it’s programming, school studies, or other subjects you are passionate about, you can organize study sessions or join ones hosted by others who share your interests.

## **Our Mission**

In recent years, learning platforms have become increasingly accessible, making it easier for individuals to study on their own. However, many people still experience setbacks when learning solo.
 Why is it so easy to give up on individual learning? Some reasons include:

* No one to consult when facing difficulties
* Feeling of loneliness when studying alone
* Struggling to maintain motivation

I, too, have experienced many moments of frustration while learning programming on my own. What changed things for me was attending a programming study session I joined by chance. 
Through the session, I met peers with similar goals, which not only boosted my motivation but also broadened my perspective by learning about different ways of thinking.

However, many people find it difficult to participate in study sessions if they don’t already belong to a community. That’s why, through this service, I aim to support you in meeting others with similar goals, sharing knowledge, and expanding your horizons.
